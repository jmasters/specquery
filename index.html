About the Pipeline

This archive contains extragalactic HI spectra observed with the Green Bank Telescope (GBT). The spectra were processed by the GBT Spectral Pipeline , a tool that generates calibrated, quick-look spectra intended for assessment of data quality. Those interested in using these data for science measurements should acquire processed data from the PI of the project, or should obtain the public raw data from the NRAO archive and process the data on their own. The recommended package for processing GBT data is GBTIDL .

This archive is currently populated by spectra observed since 2004 that used the standard total power observing mode. Earlier data may become available here at a later time. Of note, HI spectra from the "GBT HI galaxy survey" observed during GBT commissioning in 2001/2002 are currently available.

If you have any questions or suggestions relating to the archive or the GBT spectral pipeline, please contact Joe Masters (jmasters at nrao.edu) or Jim Braatz (jbraatz at nrao.edu).
